Access Keys

Access keys are designed to help people who may have difficulty using a pointing device, such as a mouse. They are keyboard shortcuts which provide a different from of navigation. Pressing the 'alt key' and another key at the same time will preform a certain function. For example, 'alt' and 'I' will submit whatever you have entered or selected on a page. The access keys you can use appear in brackets throughout the system.

Do you want to use the access keys? You can change your decision at any time.
